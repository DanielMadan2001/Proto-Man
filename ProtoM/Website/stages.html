Paint Plant

Boss: Paint Man

Key Points:

  • Heavy emphasis on hazard projectiles, mostly beams of paint.
  • All of the enemies use the corrosive paint in some way, from their projectiles to their bodies.
  • Several consecutive rooms of platforming while dodging beams.
    • Mix of the death race from Quick Man with the level design of Cut Man.
    • Final room starts with a beam that the player must slide under.
  • Boss arena has upper platforms inaccessible to the player unless they use the Motor Kick jump. By being under them, the player has shelter against the Paint Flood attack. Getting on the high ground allows the player to attack Paint Man during his attack cycles, but they are also susceptible to Paint Flood.

Important Items:

  • M Tank

  • Put here
